As part of its fall 2016 schedule Red Bull Studios New York City is producing the exhibition entitled “Total Proof” The GALA Committee 1995 – 1997” which will run from September 30th through November 27th, 2016. This will be the first comprehensive New York presentation of The GALA Committee’s In The Name Of The Place, a covert conceptual artwork deployed on the primetime television show Melrose Place from 1995-97. The GALA Committee’s site specific intervention with Melrose Place is one of the most elaborate and well orchestrated collaborations in contemporary art and television history.
